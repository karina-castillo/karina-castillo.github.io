Greenovation

Greenovation is an R&D start-up based in the O'Higgins Region, Chile. We aim to develop sustainable biotechnological solutions using vegetal raw materials available in the region, such as wild native forest plants and discarded goods from fruit, crop, and forestry activities.

Project: "Development of new healthy foods from michay fruits (Berberis darwinii H.)". Project founded by Corfo's "Sumate a innovar" instrument, in collaboration with the CECTA laboratory of the University of Santiago de Chile.

Phoenix DB

PhoenixDB is an bioinformatic tool centralizing genotyping data from the most popular date palm varieties from traditional cultivation areas and others Phoenix species. You also find highly efficient tools allowing Phoenix cultivar certification and species identification.

Patents

Molecular markers and methods for identifying date palm genotypes
The present invention concerns two sets of mini- and micro-satellite molecular markers, and the use thereof to study the genetic diversity and/or identify the genotypes of date palms. The invention also concerns method for identifying date palm cultivars using these sets of molecular markers, and kits for implementing these methods (Pub.No.:US2015/0337395A1).
